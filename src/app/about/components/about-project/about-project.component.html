In short

Open Source MEAN, ngrx and nebular based quiz platform. Made by developer for developers.

Features

  • Explanation of answers
  • Comments on answers
  • Answers and total score stats
  • Sharing result with friends
  • Social login
  • Propose your own question or answer (In Progress)
  • Create your own quiz (In Progress)

Motivation

  • Playground for latest features/libraries
  • Expierence in full-cycle development of real-life project
  • Expierence in open-source project
  • Sharing knowleage
