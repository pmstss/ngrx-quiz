In short

MEAN, ngrx and nebular based quiz platform. Made by developer for developers. Open source.

Motivation

There are existing generic quiz platforms, but didn't find the one specific for developers and their needs (code highlighting, etc).
Also:
  • Playground for latest features/libraries
  • Expierence in full-cycle development of real-life project
  • Expierence in open-source project
  • Sharing knowleage

Features

  • Explanation of answers
  • Comments on answers
  • Answers and total score stats
  • Sharing result with friends
  • Social login
  • Propose your own question or answer *
  • Create your own quiz *
* - under construction
